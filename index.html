PERSON OF THE WEEK

Veni Kunche has worked as a Software Engineer for over 15 years, now her main goal is to make the tech industry more inclusive. In 2018, she launched Diversify Tech , a collection of resources, scholarships, events, and job opportunities for underrepresented people in tech and everyone who wants to make the tech industry a welcoming and inclusive place. She also runs the newsletter Code with Veni with at least fifty percent of the featured content coming from underrepresented folks. You can find Veni on Twitter as @venikunche.
